ALPKIT GIVES BACK

Spending time outdoors is good for the mind, body and soul. But generally people are spending less time outdoors.

Alpkit Foundation is the independent charity set up by Alpkit to help people Go Nice Places Do Good Things. It supports grass-roots direct action projects that tackle issues such as diversity & inclusion in the outdoors, participation, education, conservation and protection of our natural environment and health & well-being.

Alpkit donates 1% of sales and at least 10% of annual profit to support grass roots projects. We’re incredibly proud to see the effect that these small steps can make.
